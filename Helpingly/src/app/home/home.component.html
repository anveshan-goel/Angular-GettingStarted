Interest

Classifying an individual according to one's interests so that one can be matched with appropriate careers. All careers can be classified into six broad areas, which can be used to describe people, their personalities and interests.

Career Categorization

Organizing Careers into a clearly defined set of groups according to the jobs commonly clustered within a career emphasis. Content of defined work within a job is the key criteria in determining relationship to this familial structure.

Stream-Wise

Careers are listed stream wise, hence providing all the career options emerging from a specific stream. For that matter students can be broadly classified in terms of streams namely commerce, science and arts.

Psychometric Analysis

Psychometric tests is a scientific method to check existing skills, interests and assess personality of an individual. Career counsellors use a psychometric assessment to understand the personality and interests of the candidate and assess their skill set to find a suitable Career Path
